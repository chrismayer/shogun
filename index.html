SHOGun

SHOGun, a Java based WebGIS framework.

This site is under construction

SHOGun is based on several high-quality Open Source frameworks, such as

  • Spring http://www.springsource.org/
  • Hibernate http://hibernate.org
  • OpenLayers http://openlayers.org
  • GeoExt2 http://geoext.github.io/geoext2

and serves you a qualitative middleware for managing your WebGIS applications
